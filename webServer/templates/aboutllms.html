About Large Language Models (LLMs)

What Are LLMs?

Large language models (LLMs) are AI systems capable of understanding and generating human-like text. They analyze vast amounts of data to generate responses for various language tasks.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Can perform language tasks like summarization and question answering.
  • Cons: May produce incorrect or biased information and require significant computational resources.

Popular LLMs

ChatGPT

Developed by OpenAI, ChatGPT provides detailed and context-aware conversational capabilities.

Llama

Created by Meta AI, Llama is an efficient model designed for scalable text processing.

Gemini

Google's Gemini aims to integrate multi-modal AI features for complex problem-solving.
